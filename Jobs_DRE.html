Unitarian Universalist Fellowship of Midland Position Opening

We are an inclusive liberal religious community of 150 members and friends with 35 children and youth.

Position: Director of Religious Education

Part time: 20 hrs/wk (6 units) including Sunday mornings.

Start Date: July 2017

Goal:

Looking for an outgoing, motivated, self-directed individual with religious education experience to provide professional leadership for the Religious Education Program for our children, youth and coordinate with Lifespan programming.

Responsibilities:

Through collaboration with the Religious Education Committee,

  • Recruit, orient and support volunteers.
  • Provide leadership for goal setting and development of religious education program
  • Publicize and advocate program through newsletter and other communication modalities.
  • Coordinate curriculum.
  • Maintain, budget expense, files and records for Religious Education program.
  • Attend meetings of Religious Education Committee, and additional meetings as need arises.

Key Abilities:

  • LEADERSHIP skills to motivate, recruit and positively influence others and to delegate when appropriate.
  • COMMUNICATIONS skills including timely written and verbal, plus attentive listening skills working with children, their parents and other congregants.
  • INTERPERSONAL skills to interact effectively and compassionately with our diverse community.
  • OPEN-MINDEDNESS towards the diverse beliefs of our congregants and teaching the values of Unitarian Universalism.

Preferred Experience:

  • Experience with Unitarian Universalism.
  • Religious education or education experience with children.
  • Exposure and comfort with wide variety of world religions and diverse religious belies.

Compensation: Salary with benefits. We support LREDA credentialing or plans to credential.

Applications requested to be submitted by May 15, 2017; interviews begin May 1, 2017. Submit application including, cover letter, resume and three references with contact information.
